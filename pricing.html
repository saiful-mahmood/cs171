Pricing Plans

Each line represents a decile (10%) of the customers in a pricing plan. The top decile is excluded to prevent outliers skewing the axes. High value outliers are also excluded from detail charts for the same reason.

Hover over a colored line to emphasize the lines for that plan. Click on a line to update the detail charts. This will reset the selection to all pricing plans.
