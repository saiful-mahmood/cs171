About Project

This case study is based on Onollo's dataset which currently includes over 10,000 stores. As we have launched the paid version of the product, we want to identify the most active power-users, and offer them a paid version of the product. For this reason, we are using data exploration and visualization to identify the customer personas (Producer, Distributor, and Agency) that our audience (Onollo's CEO, Marketing Team, and UX Researcher) should focus on to keep up with the demand for the paid version of the product. The key questions to consider as you move through this dashboard are:

  1. Where are Onollo's customers located?
  2. What do they sell?
  3. Who are Onollo's power users?
