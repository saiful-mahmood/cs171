Recommendations

Each one of the pages in this case study has lesson for Onollo. The lessons point to the segments, countries, and platforms where the company should focus their efforts, as well as a suggestion to improve profitability. These are eCom Gurus' recommendations to Onollo.
  1. Focus on the largest customer segments.

    Onollo should pursue companies in the apparel, beauty & fitness, and home & garden industries. As the largest customer segments, companies in these industries have shown that they value the services that Onollo provides.

    • Customer Segments, Engagement


  2. Center on high-GDP, English speaking countries.

    Onollo has the most customers in the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia. Outside that group, India has the largest number of customers. When excluding the US, Europe is the region with the most companies with 11 countries, including the UK, having more than 20 customers.

    • Geography


  3. Concentrate on the Shopify platform.

    While there are also customers from the BigCommerce platform, the amount is insignificant. Again, the companies from the Shopify platform have shown they value Onollo’s services.

    • KPIs


  4. Consider additional pricing tiers for customers with the largest number of posts.

    A small number of customers have published over 20,000 posts. Their posts make up half of Onollo’s total posts, but the revenue from these customers is only 5%. New pricing tiers could aim for 20% of revenue from customers in the top half of published posts.

    • Pricing Plans
