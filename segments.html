Customer Segments

These visualizations offer two looks at customer segments. The first looks at the top ten industry categories in order of the number of Onollo customers. The second shows the characteristics of companies by the date they joined Onollo.

Industry Categories

Below is a breakdown of the top ten customer segments of current Onollo customers. The barcharts show a visual representation of the number of current Onollo customers in a given category and some sales statistics on the right-hand side. Hover over the different bars to understand the dollar figure influence on Onollo from each market sector.

Customer Usage

Brush over the area chart to select a specific date range during which customers joined Onollo. The bar charts on the right represent the number of current customers with a specified range of products, are in the specified plan level, and the number of posts they have made.
