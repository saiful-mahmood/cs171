Engagement

The following chart visualizes the relationship between the store's inventory size (products in store) and the average amount of postings (average published posts). We can see that smaller stores, those with fewer products, usually publish fewer postings as well. Therefore, the stores with larger catalogs publish more often. The size of the piecharts reflects the number of companies in that bin. Using pie charts and the table, on the right side, we can see segments of what type of products those stores are selling (store segments). It allows us to understand the product offerings of our users.
